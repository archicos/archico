Most Outstanding? Nice Try, dude..

Most Outstanding Student of IT Del. Yes... that title has always been ringing in my head for a long time. It's something I've dreamed of for a long time, because it started with a senior high school figure who was suddenly reported to have won the title of Most Outstanding Student of ITS Surabaya. Participating in various activities, being active in various organizations, trying everything that is sometimes even very far from the limits of one's abilities.

Until that day arrived, it turned out that my name appeared as one of the six selected candidates. I felt very happy to be able to touch that point, after previously being pessimistic and having given up hope. So in that one night, I reached out to everyone I could reach to be able to give me the voting rights they had. However, it turned out that these efforts were insignificant compared to other candidates who were much better known than me, who was only known to two or three people around me.

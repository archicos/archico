PAUD MASA DEPAN CERAH

I joined kindergarten when I was 4 years old. At that time, kindergarten was supposed to last for 3 years, starting from playgroup class, then in the second year going to class A, then in the third year going to class B, and then after that you could graduate from kindergarten. However, when I joined, I immediately placed myself in class A, and after finishing class A, I immediately graduated from kindergarten and continued to elementary school. The teacher at that time didn't agree, because they didn't think I was worthy to graduate and didn't think I was old enough to go to school. However, my parents said that I could already write and read, therefore I was fit and ready to go to elementary school.

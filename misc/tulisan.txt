# Most Outstanding Student of ITDel

Most Outstanding Student of IT Del. Ya... gelar itu yang selalu terngiang ngiang di kepalaku sejak lama. Hal yang aku impikan dari jauh jauh hari, karena berawal dari sosok senior SMA yang mendadak kabarnya sudah menyandang gelar Most Outstanding Student of ITS Surabaya. Dari situlah titik awal aku memiliki mimpi ini. Sehingga satu per satu aku persiapkan sejak awal supaya bisa dinyatakan eligible mengikuti hal tersebut. Mengikuti berbagai kegiatan, aktif di berbagai organisasi, mencoba segala hal yang bahkan kadang sangat jauh dari batas kemampuan.

Hingga tiba di hari itu, ternyata namaku muncul menjadi satu dari enam kandidat yang terpilih. Sangat bahagia rasanya dapat menyentuh titik itu, setelah sebelumnya sudah pesimis dan sudah menyerah untuk berharap. Sehingga dalam waktu satu malam itu, aku menggapai semua orang yang bisa kugapai untuk dapat memberikan hak suara yang mereka punya untukku. Namun ternyata usaha tersebut tidak berarti dibandingkan dengan kandidat lain yang jauh lebih dikenal sosoknya dibanding aku yang hanya dikenal dua tiga orang saja di sekitaranku.

Most Outstanding Student of IT Del. Yes... that title has always been ringing in my head for a long time. It's something I've dreamed of for a long time, because it started with a senior high school figure who was suddenly reported to have won the title of Most Outstanding Student of ITS Surabaya. That's the starting point for me having this dream. So one by one I prepared from the start so that I could be declared eligible to take part in this. Participating in various activities, being active in various organizations, trying everything that is sometimes even very far from the limits of one's abilities.

Until that day arrived, it turned out that my name appeared as one of the six selected candidates. I felt very happy to be able to touch that point, after previously being pessimistic and having given up hope. So in that one night, I reached out to everyone I could reach to be able to give me the voting rights they had. However, it turned out that these efforts were insignificant compared to other candidates who were much better known than me, who was only known to two or three people around me.
